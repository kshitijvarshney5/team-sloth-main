Services we provide

CashCraft is a comprehensive web application designed to help users track their personal finances and manage expenses effectively. With a user-friendly interface and a range of powerful features, FinTrack streamlines financial management and provides valuable insights to achieve financial goals.

Key Features :

1. User Registration and Authentication:

  • Securely register and create an account to access the application.
  • Authenticate users to ensure data privacy and protection

2.Expense Tracking:

  • Easily record and categorize expenses in real-time.
  • Set custom spending categories and subcategories for better organization.
  • Attach receipts or invoices for future reference.

3.Budget Management:

  • Create personalized budgets based on income and expense categories.
  • Monitor budget progress with visual representations and detailed reports.

4.Financial Insight:

  • Generate reports and visualizations to understand financial habits.
  • Analyze income vs. expenses, savings ratio, and other key metrics.
